Landscapes

These are landscapes made with mostly just a traditional generative process, cycleGAN and pix2pix are used essentially only for color. Plexiglass was chosen to present these since it still retains some qualities of a screen.

The two landscapes immediately below are 16.4 x 25.1 cm (6 1/2 x 9 7/8 in) and 16.4 x 21.2 cm (6 1/2 x 8 3/8 in). Other physical productions of this series of landscapes are similarly sized. Not all of these landscapes have been or will be physically produced.

All images can be clicked to enlarge.
