Acne Studios FW2020

In 2019, Acne Studios reached out to me after seeing my Balenciaga Fan Project, and invited me to go to Stockholm to begin a collaboration with them on their Men's Fall-Winter 2020 collection. I'm very grateful to have been able to work with them, and I'm very happy with our work together. A lot of the work I did for them was initially developing references / silhouettes, and then later on in our collaboration developing tools (drawing a lot from the "corrections" process I had earlier developed from my work with Ronan Barrot, except adapted to clothing (where a designer could manually click/control areas of a garment that would be re-worked by a neural network).
