Landscapes After Gardening Software (Saint Nazaire)

These landscapes came from a direct continuation with my work with gardening software.

I think it's the first time that I was making personal work just about my life and not a technical trick or aesthetic question.
Originally I wrote; "finally accepting that not every image has to be a trick new way to use AI or a new answer to some question about digital artifacts. its nice to do both, but its also okay to just make images that are about my life"

saint nazaire
"sharp exercise landscape"
Some work based off of this was also shown on a holographic display at l'Avant Galerie's 2021 show "De la Tulipe à la Crypto Marguerite". Depending on which angle the viewer was at compared to the piece, the image would completely change.
